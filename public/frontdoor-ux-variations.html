Front door — interaction prototype

⚠ A romance-campaign INTERACTION prototype on a Wingman skin. The romance parts (red names) ship on a romance-first campaign — The Long Hunt — because the real Wingman is a coaching campaign with no romanceable NPC (story-engine rule 11). See docs/design/the-long-hunt-front-door.md.

Tap Nico for a first-impression — each popover has a ? for their look (a portrait sheet; the gallery seed). Engage the read (Actually— / Let him continue) → your first mechanic (a bottom sheet that explains the game) with the achievement unlocked at its foot — tap it for the achievement card; the 🏆 up top collects them (like the 🎒 mechanics). A name in red is a romanceable peer (oriented; never a coach). Top-right romance toggles it off. Then see the What-Changes matrix ↓.

Everyone has an orientation (depth, low-key). A romanceable peer open to you simply reads in red — a door that’s open, never a slot to fill (it never tallies into a count), never the coach (dating your coach would invert “readies you for real people”). Oriented, not playersexual: authored from character truth, so a given pronoun may find no match here (honest, not engineered for coverage). Romance is opt-in + the top-right romance toggle turns it off entirely. Tap a red name → romance mechanic + achievement.
